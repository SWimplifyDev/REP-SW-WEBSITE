Overview

Take control of your motors with QuadPower, a robust and efficient motor driver board designed for high-power applications. Whether you're building robots, CNC machines, or automation systems, QuadPower ensures precise motor control, high reliability, and powerful performance.

Key Features

  • Control 4 Motors Independently – Perfect for robotics, automation, and industrial applications.
  • High-Power Support – Operates with motor voltages up to 46V and delivers 2A continuous per channel (3A peak).
  • Dual L298P H-Bridge Drivers – Proven and reliable motor driver ICs for stable performance.
  • PWM Speed Control – Adjust motor speed dynamically using a Pulse Width Modulation (PWM) signal.
  • Direction Control – Set motor direction using simple logic inputs.
  • Current Sensing Output – Monitor real-time current consumption with an analog output ranging from -1V to 2.3V.
  • Compact and Efficient Design – Optimized for minimal heat generation and maximum performance.
  • Plug & Play Connectivity – Screw terminals and header pins for easy integration into your projects.

Technical Specifications

  • Motor Voltage (VS) : Up to 46V
  • Logic Voltage (VSS) : 5V (Microcontroller Compatible)
  • Continuous Current : 2A per channel
  • Peak Current : 3A (Short Pulses)
  • Motor Channels : 4 Independent Outputs
  • Control Inputs : PWM & Logic (IN1, IN2, EN)
  • Current Sensing : Analog Output (-1V to 2.3V)
  • Protection : Flyback Diodes
  • Board Size : Compact & Space-Efficient
